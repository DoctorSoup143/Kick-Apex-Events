About

The Kalashnikov Clash is a solo event that includes the maps Customs, Interchange, Reserve, and Shorline. During the event, Customs will be played twice - once at the beginning and once at the end. This free-for-all competition will feature 12 competitors vying for the highest score. Points will be awarded for both survival and PMC kills. To ensure fairness for competitors of all PMC levels, certain ammo and items are restricted.

Note: This event will be played on US Servers.
